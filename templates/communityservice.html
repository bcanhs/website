Community Service

Steps to Propose an NHS Community Service Event:

  1. Ensure that there are at least 10 members participating in your event, and that there is a way to track attendance for the event
  2. Fill out the NHS Community Service Proposals Form
  3. Done! - your proposal will be considered by the board, you will get a response in 1-2 weeks

Steps to Propose an NHS Community Service Event:

  1. If required, sign up for the event
  2. Participate in the event for at least 4 hours total, make sure to sign in
  3. Fill out the NHS Community Service Completion Form
  4. Done! - we will verify your participation and log your hours

Questions? Ask your officer.
