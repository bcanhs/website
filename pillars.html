Membership Requirements

The following are requirements for anyone wishing to apply to the BCA Chapter of the NHS:

  • Scholarship: Minimum 3.500 GPA at the end of sophomore/junior year as reflected on Naviance (3.499 is not 3.500).
  • Leadership: Demonstrated leadership in and out of school with leadership qualities, skills, and experience (e.g. Vice President of Class Council).
  • Service: At least 40 completed hours of community service for applying juniors and seniors (only 10 hours can be completed in-house).
  • Character: Demonstrating trustworthiness, respect, responsibility, fairness, caring, and citizenship (student complies with school regulations, avoids cheating, and takes criticism willingly).
